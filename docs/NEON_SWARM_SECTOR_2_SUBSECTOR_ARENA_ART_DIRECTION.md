# Neon Swarm Sector 2 Subsector Arena Art Direction

Sector 2 is Prism Rift: a purple, magenta, and violet fractured prism/glass
arena family with dark sci-fi rift mood and readable modeled floor faces.

Phase 49 keeps the approved 2.0 Prism Gate arena as the base and adds distinct
visual-only full arena-layout variants for 2A through 2D.

Primary user reference:

- `art/reference/sector_2_prism_rift/sector_2_reference_sheet.png`

Reference panel mapping:

- Panel 1: 2.0 Prism Gate / base approved arena.
- Panel 2: 2A Mirror Flats / reflective prism floor.
- Panel 3: 2B Fracture Hall / cracked hall.
- Panel 4: 2C Violet Glassway / long glassway route.
- Panel 5: 2D Rift Lens / final focusing lens approach.

The sheet is design intent, not a texture source. The implementation uses
modeled 3D forms: plates, bays, ribs, supports, glass inserts, prism wells,
focal rings, side structures, and readable boundary architecture.

## Shared Rules

- Stay in the Sector 2 Prism Rift family.
- Use dark violet/gunmetal support, amethyst glass, magenta fracture cores,
  violet glass sheen, and restrained cyan counter-accents.
- Keep floor faces visible; do not let glass collapse into black.
- Keep neon embedded in structures rather than replacing the structures.
- Do not build subsectors as random line overlays, random box clusters, flat
  purple boards, or recolored copies of the same room.
- Preserve player, XP, enemies, bullets, ripple, Lyra panel, HUD, boss cards,
  Memory Shard panel, and reward UI readability.

## 2A Mirror Flats

Story purpose: the floor and enemies distort into reflected patterns.

Visual language:

- Broad mirrored prism floor plates.
- Controlled symmetry.
- Shallow reflective glass panels.
- Side mirror rails and horizontal header structures.
- Edge prism clusters that frame the flat reflective field.

Readability target: elegant and clean, with large reflective plate shapes
visible from the gameplay camera.

## 2B Fracture Hall

Story purpose: Nova begins hearing impossible signal echoes.

Visual language:

- Broken split floor plates.
- A central cracked glass path.
- Magenta rift split channels embedded in the fracture.
- Fractured cross ribs and wall prism ribs.
- Structural damage that reads as architecture, not random scratches.

Readability target: disrupted and dangerous without hiding enemies, XP, bullets,
or the player ripple.

## 2C Violet Glassway

Story purpose: Mira's voice becomes clearer through Prism Shards.

Visual language:

- Long deliberate glass route.
- Three circular lens nodes along the path.
- Raised violet side structures.
- Route end frames.
- Controlled magenta core line built into the glassway.

Readability target: a clear route-like arena with elegant rift structure and no
visual clutter over combat.

## 2D Rift Lens

Story purpose: final focus point before Veyraxis.

Visual language:

- Large focal lens construction.
- Central magenta eye/core.
- Radial focusing ribs.
- Prism tower/focusing structures near the corners.
- Heavier boss approach wall and route frames.

Readability target: more dramatic than prior subsectors and clearly the
approach to Veyraxis, while keeping the gameplay plane flat.
